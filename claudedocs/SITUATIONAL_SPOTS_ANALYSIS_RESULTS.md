## Detailed Findings

### 1. Back-to-Back Impact (VALIDATED)

**Overall Results (11,452 games)**:

| Situation | Games | Avg Total | vs Baseline |
|-----------|-------|-----------|-------------|
| Neither B2B (baseline) | 7,626 | 218.7 | -- |
| Home B2B only | 1,032 | 219.3 | +0.5 |
| Away B2B only | 2,039 | **216.0** | **-2.7** |
| Both B2B | 594 | **215.3** | **-3.4** |
| Home rested + Away B2B | 537 | 217.3 | -1.4 |

**Key Insight**:
- **Away team fatigue creates consistent UNDER pressure**
- When BOTH teams are fatigued, the effect compounds
- Home team on B2B shows NO negative effect (home crowd/comfort offset fatigue)

**Betting Application**:
- When away team is on B2B → slight UNDER lean (-2.7 pts adjustment)
- When BOTH teams on B2B → stronger UNDER lean (-3.4 pts adjustment)
- Ignore home team B2B in isolation

**Betting Application**:
- First 3 weeks → subtract 3 points from expected total

- Be more cautious with early-season projections (higher variance)
